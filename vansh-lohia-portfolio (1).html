The Story

From Execution
to Strategy

I didn't start with a plan. I started with a job. My early exposure in a Finance & Loan Department taught me something textbooks skip — trust is the only real product. You don't sell a loan. You sell confidence and reliability.

Then came Reliance. Ground-level sales. Real rejections. Real conversions. I learned that revenue isn't a slide in a pitch deck — it's built through daily discipline, customer empathy, and ruthless execution.

That experience sparked a question: why do most businesses struggle to grow despite having great products? The answer was almost always marketing. So I built Glorify Digital — a digital marketing agency helping brands grow through SEO, Social Media, Paid Ads, and Content Strategy.

Today I'm a BBA student (2025–28) preparing for CAT 2027 with a clear destination: a top IIM, then MBB consulting. But the journey isn't paused — it's accelerating.

"I believe in execution over theory, clarity over noise, and building things that create real impact."

01
Gym Client Lead Growth
3×
Leads in 30 Days
Problem
Local gym struggling with stagnant walk-ins despite strong offline reputation. Zero structured digital presence.
Strategy
Built a full-funnel Instagram presence with transformation content, local hashtag targeting, and a referral incentive loop.
Execution

Client · eCommerce
02
eCommerce Brand Sales Growth
110%
Instagram-Driven Sales
Problem
eCommerce brand with good products but no social strategy. Instagram was dormant, website traffic minimal, and repeat purchases rare.
Strategy
Repositioned brand voice, built a consistent visual identity, and designed a content system mixing product posts, UGC-style reels, and story funnels.
Execution
Weekly content drops, DM automation for warm leads, bio link optimisation, and retargeting ads for cart abandoners.
Result
110% increase in Instagram-attributed sales. Follower engagement tripled. Profile visits up 4× within 45 days.
